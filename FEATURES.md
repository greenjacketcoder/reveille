## Key Advantages of MacAlert

1. **Completely Free** - No trial period, no payments, no strings attached
2. **Open Source** - Modify, improve, and customize to your needs
3. **Privacy First** - All data stays on your Mac, no cloud services
4. **No Vendor Lock-in** - Own your meeting reminder system
5. **Community Driven** - Contribute features you want to see

## Installation Comparison

**Chime:**
- Download from website
- 14-day trial
- Requires payment after trial

**MacAlert:**
- Build from source in Xcode (5 minutes)
- Use forever, for free
- No account or payment info needed

## What You Get Out of the Box

MacAlert gives you 95% of Chime's features without spending $50-$200:
- ✅ Full-screen meeting alerts
- ✅ Calendar integration (all providers)
- ✅ Reminders support
- ✅ Custom sounds and timing
- ✅ Quick event creation
- ✅ Personal meeting links
- ✅ Professional settings UI

The only missing features are third-party integrations (Todoist) and analytics, which most users don't need for basic meeting reminders.
